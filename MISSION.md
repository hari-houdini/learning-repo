# Mission: Interview-Ready AI Product Engineer

## Why
Hari (TypeScript engineer, 4–6 yrs, needs UK sponsorship) is failing interviews at every stage. Diagnosis: a scattered profile with no identity, a spoken-articulation deficit, and no buildable AI evidence despite AI questions dominating interviews. The goal is a sponsored mid/senior offer within 3–6 months by becoming a standout **AI product engineer — TypeScript full-stack (React + Node) who ships agentic AI features**.

## Success looks like
- Passes mock interview loops at "hire" for mid and "lean hire+" for senior across coding, system design, and behavioral
- Answers "have you used multi-agent AI?" with a demo, an architecture story, and trade-off opinions from his own portfolio project
- Tells 8–10 drilled STAR stories that evidence senior signals; can define and evidence "senior" without hesitation
- Opens every system design with 5 minutes of sharp clarifying questions before drawing anything
- CV rewritten around the identity; funnel tracker shows screens-per-application improving; offer signed

## Constraints
- 12 hrs/week floor (employed full-time); syllabus sized to hours, never the reverse
- Dual-track: real interviews continue throughout — they are the feedback loop, not an interruption
- Every study session ends with recorded spoken output; passive input alone is banned
- 24-week horizon starting 2026-07-07 (offers targeted from ~October 2026)

## Out of scope
- "Advanced everything" — depth follows the 3-tier map in [curriculum/00-overview.md](curriculum/00-overview.md)
- Rebranding as a pure frontend or pure backend specialist
- More than ONE portfolio project
- Non-TypeScript ecosystems (Python ML, Go, etc.) beyond conversational awareness
